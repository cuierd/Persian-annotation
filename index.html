Select Distractors for Persian Words

In each box, the first line shows the beginning of a sentence that ends with the target word. The second line shows the target word itself. The third line shows six possible distractor words: words that make the sentence IMPLAUSIBLE or INCORRECT.

Your task is to choose the best distractor: the word that would make the sentence most implausible compared with the original target word. If all distractor options are placeholders such as “XXX”, you may choose any one of them. If several repeated options are good distractors, randomly choose one of them. If several different options are good distractors, choose the most implausible option, especially one that is grammatically incorrect or clearly does not fit the sentence.

If none of the options are good, choose “None” or type your own suggestion in the “Others” box and click “Use custom”. After finishing all entries, download a backup TSV file and then click “Submit responses”.
